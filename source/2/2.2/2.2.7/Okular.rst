Chapter 2.2.7 Okular
====================
Okular is the pdf viewer for Lubuntu 24,04 

Usage
-----

Version
-------
Lubuntu ships with version 23.08.4 of Okular.

How to Launch
-------------

To launch Okular :menuselection:`Internet --> Okular` or run 

.. code::

   okular
   
from the command line.
