Chapter 2.2.3 LibreOffice Draw
==============================

Libreoffice Draw is the drawing portion of LibreOffice.

Usage
-----
To draw a line left click on the :guilabel:`single line` button on the toolbar on the left. Then click at one point where you want the line to start and move with the mouse to where you want it to end.

Version
-------
Lubuntu ships with version 7.5.6 of Libreoffice Draw.

How to Launch
-------------

To launch LibreOffice Draw :menuselection:`Office --> LibreOffice Draw` or run ..code ::

    lodraw
    
from the comand line.
